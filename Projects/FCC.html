freeCodeCamp

I began working through Free Code Camp’s curriculum in the summer of 2016. A big thing that drew me into it was the idea of that when you are done, you have the option to do pro bono work for nonprofit organizations. I feel like that’s the big thing that makes Free Code Camp special, when you are done with the camp, you get to use and apply the knowledge you just acquired in a very positive way.
